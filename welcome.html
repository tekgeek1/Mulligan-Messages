Welcome to Mulligan Pro ⛳️

Everything you need for a better round — free, with no catch.

Great to have you! Mulligan Pro is built by a golfer, for golfers — and it’s completely free. No ads, no paywall. Here’s how to get the most out of it:

  • 🎯Accurate GPS distances — front, center, and back of every green, plus per-club yardages.
  • 📝Easy scorecards — track score, putts, and stats hole by hole, or go GPS-only in Rangefinder mode.
  • ⌚Apple Watch — score and see distances right from your wrist, in sync with your phone.
  • 🌐Thousands of courses — find yours in Nearby, or search by name from anywhere.
  • ❤️Apple Health — your rounds count toward your activity automatically.

💡 Tip: On the course? Tap Play, pick a round mode, and Mulligan finds your course automatically. On the green, it shows a clean “Putter” pill so there’s no clutter when you’re putting.

Don’t see a course you play?

New courses get added all the time — and you can help. If a course you play isn’t in Mulligan yet, send it my way and I’ll get it added fast: mulligangolfpros@gmail.com or use the feedback option right in the app.
